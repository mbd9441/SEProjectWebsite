Requirements

The first step in our project, as with any, was requirements gathering. All we had to start off with to gather requirements were [this document] We compiled a list of these requirements, but that list was quite short. We knew we had to come up with a more detailed list. This was initially done by asking questions with our sponsor to try to clarify things. We got together a much more extensive list, however, there was still some ambiguity and clarification needed. We decided that imagery would be most useful as it can depict a bit more than just words, and we understood our sponsor was keen on design as well. We drew up a few wireframes very early on to try to map out the flow and design of the system. We presented this to Bizcloud as a talking point and this allowed us to discuss how we imagined the system would work vs what Bizcloud was thinking about it.
